Research

Patient Perceptions of Blockchain-Based Personal Health Records

Qualitative insights into security, trust, efficiency, and data control when blockchain is introduced into Personal Health Record systems.
Preprint · December 2025 · University of the Cumberlands

This blockchain-based study uses interpretative phenomenological analysis (IPA) to understand how patients perceive the value of blockchain-enabled PHRs. Themes include perceived security and transparency, usability and digital literacy barriers, and the importance of patient-centred control over data access and sharing.

PHR Vault – 2026 Prototype

A decentralized PHR system demonstrating patient-controlled data access using Web3 technologies (Solidity, IPFS, smart contracts).

The PHR Vault prototype is designed as a technical companion to the research, translating theoretical insights on trust, security, and patient agency into an experimental implementation. Future work includes UX refinement, multi-chain support, and integration with existing healthcare workflows.
